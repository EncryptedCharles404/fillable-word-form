12 — Complaint Procedure

Integrated Sycamore Group LLC is committed to resolving all employee and consumer concerns promptly and fairly. Follow these steps:

  1. Informal Resolution: Speak directly with your immediate supervisor or Program Manager within 24 hours of the incident to attempt informal resolution.
  2. Written Complaint: If unresolved, submit a written complaint to the Program Director within 5 business days. Include all relevant dates, individuals involved, and a description of the concern.
  3. Management Review: The Program Director will acknowledge receipt within 2 business days and conduct a formal review within 10 business days, interviewing involved parties as necessary.
  4. Written Response: A written determination will be provided to all parties within 15 business days of receipt of the formal complaint.
  5. External Escalation: If the matter remains unresolved after internal review, the complainant may contact DADS (Department of Aging and Disability Services) or HHSC directly.
Note: Complaints regarding consumer care, rights violations, or regulatory non-compliance may also be reported directly to DADS at any time without first exhausting internal procedures.
